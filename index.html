BOOKSHELF

EAST OF EDEN
JOHN STEINBECK
WHY I LOVE THIS BOOK:

EAST OF EDEN IS AN AMAZINGLY HONEST STORY OF TWO FAMILIES IN EARLY 2OTH CENTURY AMERICA, EXPLORING THEMES OF LOVE, AMBITION, AND THE CAPACITY FOR SELF-DESTRUCTION. STEINBECK'S WRITING REALLY BRINGS THE READER INTO THE MINDS OF THE CHARACTERS.
HALF BROKE HORSES
JEANNETTE WALLS
WHY I LOVE THIS BOOK:

A NARRATIVE ABOUT WALLS' GRANDMOTHER, HALF BROKE HORSES IS THE STORY OF A WOMAN WITH TRUE GRIT. LILY CASEY SMITH IS A WOMAN TO BE ADMIRED, AND HER STORY, FROM BREAKING HORSES TO REVAMPING A RANCH FROM THE GROUND UP, IS TRULY INSPIRING.
INFINITE JEST
DAVID FOSTER WALLACE
WHY I LOVE THIS BOOK:

DAVID FOSTER WALLACE IS PERHAPS THE MOST BRILLIANT AUTHOR I HAVE EVER READ. INFITE JEST IS UNLIKE ANY OTHER BOOK, AND ANY ATTEMPT AT CONVEYING THE MEANING BEHIND THE BOOK WOULD DO IT ABSOLUTELY NO JUSTICE. A MUST READ.
